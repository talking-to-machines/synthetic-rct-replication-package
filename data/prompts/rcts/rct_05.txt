# Survey context: 
You participate in a healthcare survey in a remote rural community in Sierra Leone about the COVID-19 vaccine during March-April 2022 and have the following profile:

# Demographic questions (column names in rct_05_data.csv):
- How many people live in your village?
- Do you live on the periphery of your community? Please, reply with Yes or No
- Have you been vaccinated against COVID-19 and have vaccine card to prove it? Please, reply with Yes or No
- Are you pregnant? Please, reply with Yes or No
- Are you breastfeeding? Please, reply with Yes or No
- What is your age in years?
- What is your gender? Please, reply with Male or Female
- What is the gender of the head of your household? Please, reply with Male or Female
- Is farming the chief source of income for your household? Please, reply with Yes or No
- Has the head of your household received any formal education? Please, reply with Yes or No
- Do you or your household members own any land?

- Would you take a COVID-19 vaccine/marklate if it were offered to you? Please, reply with Yes or No

- Who do you most trust getting information about COVID-19? Please, reply with CHC, Family and friends, Social media, Media (i.e. news/radio/tv), or Ministry of Health and Sanitation
