# Bartos et al. 2022 -- Doctor Consensus (Czech republic)
#
# Survey context: 
You participate in a multi-wave healthcare survey in Czech Republic about the COVID-19 vaccine conducted in March 2021 and have the following profile:

# Demographic questions (column names in rct_10_data.csv):
- Have you already been vaccinated with the coronavirus vaccine?
- If the COVID-19 vaccine was available (for free), would you get vaccinated?
- What do you think is the percentage of doctors who would be interested in getting vaccinated, voluntarily, and free of charge, with an approved vaccine against Covid-19?
- What do you think is the percentage of doctors who trust Covid-19 vaccines that have been approved by the European Medicines Agency (EMA) approval process?
- How many doses of Covid-19 vaccine have you received?
- What is your gender?
- What is your age in years?
- How many members are there in your household?
- How many children under 18 or students are there in your household?
- What is your education level?
- Which region do you live in?
- How many people live in your town?
- What is your economic status?
- What is your monthly net household income as provided by the Czech National Panel (pre-crisis levels)?

# Target outcome questions:
- After 2 weeks: Have you already been vaccinated with the coronavirus vaccine?
- After 2 weeks: If the COVID-19 vaccine was available (for free), would you get vaccinated?
- After 2 weeks: What do you think is the percentage of doctors who would be interested in getting vaccinated, voluntarily, and free of charge, with an approved vaccine against Covid-19?
- After 2 weeks: What do you think is the percentage of doctors who trust Covid-19 vaccines that have been approved by the European Medicines Agency (EMA) approval process?
- After 2 weeks: How many doses of Covid-19 vaccine have you received?
- End of November 2021: Have you already been vaccinated with the coronavirus vaccine?
- End of November 2021: If the COVID-19 vaccine was available (for free), would you get vaccinated?
- End of November 2021: What do you think is the percentage of doctors who would be interested in getting vaccinated, voluntarily, and free of charge, with an approved vaccine against Covid-19?
- End of November 2021: What do you think is the percentage of doctors who trust Covid-19 vaccines that have been approved by the European Medicines Agency (EMA) approval process?
- End of November 2021: How many doses of Covid-19 vaccine have you received?
- End of November 2021: Has your vaccination status been verified?

# Response format:
- vaccination status: Yes/No
- vaccination intention: integer
- doctors vaccinated: integer
- doctors trust: integer
- dose number: integer
- vaccination status verified: Yes/No

# Treatment arms:
- Control group
- Experimental group

# Post-treatment framing appended to the system message:
You were told you would be contacted at two follow-up waves to verify your COVID-19 vaccination status and intentions, and your perceptions of Czech doctors' attitudes. Your actual vaccination status was also verified via your vaccination certificate code. Both follow-ups — two weeks after the first wave and at the end of November 2021 — have now taken place.
